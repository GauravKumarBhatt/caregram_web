CORE PRINCIPLES

Our Commitment to Holistic Care

At CareGram, we are dedicated to creating a world with compassionate, inclusive, and transformative care. Our holistic approach is guided by core principles that drive our mission:

Advocacy

We advocate for care as a fundamental right, influencing policies to prioritize caregiver support and equitable care access.

Innovation

We develop community-led models and collaborate with stakeholders, enhancing the quality and accessibility of care for diverse needs.

Education

We provide training and resources to caregivers, promoting awareness of care rights and responsibilities.

IMPACT - CURAEED CHILD CARE COMMUNITIES (C4)

Revolutionizing Childcare With
Community-Driven Solutions

C4, also known as Strawberry Fields, revolutionizes childcare with community-driven solutions.
Our social franchising model empowers caregivers and builds resilient, community-owned
childcare centers for low and middle-income families.

Enable women to lead and manage childcare centers, beyond traditional roles.

Flexible network-based approach to overcome existing model limitations.

Collaborate with service providers to ensure top-notch childcare standards.
